Rubik's Cubes

Cross: you create a white cross, but make sure the colors on the side of the white's line up with the center colors too.
F2L: You build on the corners and go until the bottom two layers are completed.
OLL: Make the yellow top be compltely white (it might not be solved yet, and that's okay)
PLL: Finish the cube! (The pros combine step 3 and 4)

Valorant: Gekko

Gekko is a character in the video game Valorant, and he's the character that I choose to play most (aka my 'main'). He has a total of four abilities: Wingman, Dizzy, Mosh, Thrash. Each ability is a friend of his, and they look like some sort of alien. But, they're super cute. Even the one named Thrash. He's special because he can use his abilities an infinite amount of times if he can pick them back up again.

Wingman:
Wingman is a little yellow alien (the one Gekko's top left shoulder) that can do anything from running into an area and clearing it out to planting and defusing a bomb. Wingman is known for being one of the cutest 'animals' in the game.

Dizzy:
Dizzy is a blue alien that can float. She gets thrown into the air, scanning an area, and if it sees an enemy, it shoots 'goop' at their face. If it hits them, they're blinded.

Mosh:
Mosh is a green alien that acts like liquid. Once thrown, Mosh creates a big field on the ground. After a short time, he explodes killing any enemy within his vicinity.
